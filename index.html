About:


I am a research scholar in the Department of Computer Science & Engineering, IIT Indore, Indore, India. I am working in the Pattern Analysis & Machine Intelligence lab under the supervision of Dr. Surya Prakash. My research interest broadly includes Computer Vision, Biometric Security, Machine Learning, and Deep Learning.

  In my PhD thesis work, I am currently working in the area of biometric template protection. My work includes the development of secure and efficient techniques to protect the user template stored in the database as the databases are vulnerable to attack which can cause an identity theft of a user. Presently, I am working towards the developement of robust techniques to secure the fingerprint user template.

  • Biometric Security
  • Machine Learning
  • Deep Learning
